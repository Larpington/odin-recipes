Air Fried Chicken Thighs

These juicy chicken thighs are healthy and appetizing!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Spices and seasonings (salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder)

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees
  2. Pat the thighs dry and brush the skins with olive oil
  3. Mix the seasonings and sprinkle it over the thighs
  4. Cook for 18 minutes (the internal temperature of the meat should be 165 degrees), flipping halfway through to cook evenly
