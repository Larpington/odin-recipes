Air Fried Hamburgers

These air fried hamburgers are are quick an easy when the weather doesn't permit outdoor grilling!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of 80% ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • freshly ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat Air Fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Form patties into 4 patties, 3/4 of an inch thick and 4 1/2 inches in diameter. Make the patties bigger to allow for shrinkage.
  3. Brush melted butter onto the patties
  4. For medium doneness, place patties into the air fryer basket and cook for 7 minutes, flipping the patties halfway through to cook evenly. For well done patties, cook for an additional 2 minutes
